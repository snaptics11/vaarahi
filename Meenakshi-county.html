A Premium Residential Community in Shankarpally

Located in Shankarpally, Sri Meenakshi County is a 101-acre HMDA & RERA-approved gated community offering premium residential plots in a strategically growing area. Surrounded by major infrastructural developments and seamlessly connected to IT hubs, educational institutions, and entertainment zones, this project is ideal for both homebuyers and investors seeking future-ready real estate.

Location :

Sri Meenakshi County is located in Shankarpally, with seamless connectivity to Mumbai Highway and key areas like Neopolis Kokapet SEZ, IIT Hyderabad, and Financial District. The project is just 30 minutes from Neopolis Kokapet and 20 minutes from IIT Hyderabad, making it a prime choice for those looking for long-term value appreciation.

Luxury Villas in Wadakpally, Near ORR, Hyderabad

Nandan Emerald is an exclusive 56-acre luxury villa community nestled in the serene surroundings of Wadakpally, near the Outer Ring Road (ORR), Hyderabad. Offering world-class living spaces, state-of-the-art amenities, and top-tier specifications, this project promises a lifestyle of comfort, luxury, and convenience for discerning homeowners.

Project Highlights:

  • Gated community ensuring legal security and peace of mind
  • 56-acre development featuring 400+ triplex villas (East & West facing)
  • Vastu-compliant design for a balanced and harmonious living environment
  • Proximity to prestigious schools, financial district, and HITECH City
  • Prime location next to ORR & Sultanpur Exit No. 4, with easy access to major city hubs
  • Luxury amenities including a clubhouse, swimming pool, gym, parks, and more
  • High-end specifications with RCC-framed structure, premium finishes, and modern interiors

Villa Configurations & Specifications:

Nandan Emerald Villas are designed with meticulous attention to detail, offering luxurious living spaces:

  • Specifications:
  • RCC-framed structure for durability
  • Teak wood doors & UPVC windows for long-lasting quality
  • Premium flooring (vitrified & wooden) for a polished finish
  • Modular kitchen with modern provisions

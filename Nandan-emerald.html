Nandan Emerald – Luxury Triplex Villas in a Premium Gated Community

Located in Beeramguda, Hyderabad, Nandan Emerald is a 56-acre gated villa community designed for those who seek a luxurious and modern lifestyle. With over 400 premium triplex villas, the project is HMDA-approved and features a wide range of state-of-the-art amenities, ensuring a high standard of living.

Project Highlights

  • HMDA-approved gated villa community
  • 4 BHK triplex villas with expansive living spaces
  • Premium specifications with top-notch construction quality
  • Vastu-compliant design ensuring positive energy
  • 24/7 security & generator backup for uninterrupted living
  • Proximity to IT hubs, major schools, and hospitals

Villa Configurations & Specifications

Nandan Emerald offers spacious triplex villas in two different plot sizes, designed to provide ample living space and comfort.

Each villa is crafted with top-quality materials and boasts modern interiors, including:

  • 200 Sq Yds (3392 Sq Ft) – Available in both East and West-facing options
  • 165 Sq Yds (2765 Sq Ft East-facing, 2701 Sq Ft West-facing)
  • Teak wood main door with elegant finishing
  • Flush doors & UPVC sliding windows for durability
  • Vitrified tile flooring in all living spaces
  • Anti-skid tiles in bathrooms for safety
  • Modular kitchen provisions for convenience
  • Weather-proof emulsion & texture paint for long-lasting aesthetics
  • Branded sanitary fittings & CPVC pipelines for efficient plumbing
  • 3-phase concealed copper wiring with power outlets for all appliances
  • Stainless steel railings inside & mild steel railings outside for safety

World-Class Amenities & Infrastructure

At Nandan Emerald, luxury extends beyond the villas. Residents can enjoy a range of exceptional amenities that enhance their quality of life:

  • Exclusive Clubhouse (Club Emerald) with modern amenities
  • Swimming pool for relaxation and fitness
  • Indoor games including carroms, chess, and table tennis
  • Fully equipped gym & yoga/meditation room
  • Library, guest rooms, and supermarket provision for convenience
  • Multi-purpose hall for social gatherings and community events
  • Jogging tracks, reflexology paths, and children’s play areas
  • 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance & generator backup
  • Wide internal roads (100', 50', 40', 33', and 30') with blacktop finish

Prime Location & Connectivity

Strategically situated near Hyderabad's major IT hubs and educational institutions, Nandan Emerald ensures seamless connectivity to key destinations:

  • Beside Silver Oaks International School
  • 5 minutes from Sultanpur Exit No. 4 on Outer Ring Road (ORR)
  • 15-30 minutes to HITEC City, Financial District & Gachibowli
  • Close to SLG Hospital & Mamata Academy of Medical Sciences
  • Near shopping centers like GSM Mall
  • Proximity to India's Largest Proposed Medical Device Park (250 acres)

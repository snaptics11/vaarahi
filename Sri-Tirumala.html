About Sri Tirumala Millennium (Phase 3)

Luxury 2BHK & 3BHK Apartments in Nacharam, Hyderabad

Sri Tirumala Millennium (Phase 3) is a premium residential project in Nacharam, Hyderabad, offering luxury 2BHK & 3BHK apartments with state-of-the-art amenities. Designed for modern living, this gated community provides exclusive comforts, excellent connectivity, and a serene environment, making it an ideal choice for families and professionals.

Project Highlights:

  • Total Area: 8 acres
  • Total Units: 700 across 3 phases (5 floors each)
  • Price: ₹5,500 per sq. ft
  • 3.6 km from Habsiguda Metro Station
  • Amenities Cost: ₹7.2 lakhs
  • Located in a prime, well-developed residential & industrial area
  • Near Infosys Raheja & NSL IT Park
  • Close proximity to leading schools, hospitals, and business hubs

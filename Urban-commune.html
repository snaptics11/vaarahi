About Urban Commune

Location Download Brochure Download Layout

Urban Commune is an exclusive 101-acre residential project located in Shankarpally, near Mumbai Highway, offering a perfect blend of modern living and tranquility. With HMDA & RERA approval, this premium gated community is designed for those who seek luxury, convenience, and connectivity in a prime location.

Project Highlights:

  • Premium gated community with 24/7 security, ensuring a safe environment
  • Vastu-compliant design for a harmonious living experience
  • Thoughtfully designed for maximum privacy and comfort
  • High appreciation potential due to rapid development in Shankarpally
  • Excellent connectivity to key hubs like IIT Hyderabad, ICFAI Business School, and Neopolis Kokapet SEZ

Nearby Landmarks & Connectivity:

  • Neopolis Kokapet SEZ – 30 Mins Drive
  • IIT Hyderabad & ICFAI Business School – 20 Mins Drive
  • BDL Township & Ordnance Factory – 10 Mins Drive
  • Indus International School – 25 Mins Drive

  • Rajiv Gandhi International Airport – Approx. 45 Mins Drive

Amenities & Features:

Urban Commune offers world-class amenities and infrastructure designed to enhance your lifestyle:

  • Gated Community with 24/7 security & CCTV surveillance
  • Underground drainage and water supply systems
  • Well-planned roads and landscaping for a serene environment
  • Fitness & wellness center, including gym and yoga space
  • Clubhouse, party hall, and recreational zones for social events
  • Children’s play area and landscaped parks for family-friendly activities
  • Rainwater harvesting systems to ensure sustainable living
  • Street lights and power backup for uninterrupted comfort

  • Teak wood doors and UPVC windows for enhanced quality

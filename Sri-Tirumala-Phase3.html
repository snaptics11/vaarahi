A Complete Community in the Heart of East Hyderabad

Sri Tirumala Millennium Phase 3 is a thoughtfully planned residential community in Nacharam, designed for families who value comfort, convenience, and quality. Spanning 8 acres and offering 700+ well-crafted units, this gated community blends spacious layouts with modern amenities, ensuring a lifestyle of ease and sophistication.

With state-of-the-art features like a 20,000 sq. ft. clubhouse, international-style swimming pool, gymnasium, and top-tier security, the project delivers an elevated urban living experience. The strategic location near educational institutions, IT parks, and metro stations makes it ideal for both homeowners and investors seeking value and connectivity.

Square Avasa – Luxury Villas in Tukkuguda, Hyderabad

Nestled in the heart of Tukkuguda, near Shamshabad Airport, Square Avasa is a premium gated villa community that redefines modern luxury living. With HMDA approval, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and proximity to key IT and aerospace hubs, this project offers an unparalleled lifestyle for homeowners and investors alike.

Project Highlights

  • HMDA-approved gated community ensuring legal security
  • Luxury 4 & 5 BHK villas with spacious floor plans
  • Vastu-compliant design to enhance positivity & harmony
  • 24/7 security & CCTV surveillance for a safe and peaceful living environment
  • Premium clubhouse & world-class amenities for leisure and fitness
  • High appreciation potential due to rapid development in the Tukkuguda region

Villa Configurations & Specifications:

Square Avasa offers a variety of luxury villa options, meticulously designed with top-quality materials and elegant interiors:

  • 200 & 240 Sq. Yds. villas with built-up areas of 2000 - 3199 Sq. Ft.
  • Vitrified & wooden flooring for a premium finish
  • Teak wood doors & UPVC windows ensuring long-lasting quality
  • RCC-framed structure for durability
  • Designer modular kitchen provisions for modern cooking convenience
  • Premium sanitary fittings & concealed wiring for safety and efficiency

World-Class Amenities & Infrastructure:

Residents of Square Avasa enjoy top-tier recreational and wellness facilities within the community, including:

  • Swimming pool for relaxation and fitness
  • Fully-equipped gym & yoga space for a healthy lifestyle
  • Outdoor & indoor sports courts (tennis, badminton, basketball, cricket nets)
  • Landscaped parks & jogging tracks for rejuvenation
  • Party halls & clubhouse for social gatherings
  • 24/7 security & generator backup for uninterrupted comfort

Prime Location & Connectivity:

Strategically located in Tukkuguda, Square Avasa offers excellent connectivity to Hyderabad's major hubs:

  • 5 minutes from Outer Ring Road (ORR) & Shamshabad Airport
  • Close to Financial District, IT hubs, and Aerospace SEZ
  • Near reputed educational institutions & hospitals
  • Proximity to Amazon Data Center, TCS, Tata Aerospace, and Foxconn
